Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Sterling Voice Orders respects the privacy of merchants and customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you install or use the App.

1. Information We Access

The App accesses Clover merchant, order, and inventory data only to enable voice-based ordering. We do not collect or store personal identifiable customer data.

2. Data Storage

No customer personal data is stored outside of Clover systems. Temporary processing of order data may occur to convert voice input into structured order data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell data to third parties. Data is only used to process restaurant orders.

4. Security

All communication with Clover is secured over HTTPS, and no sensitive data is transmitted insecurely.
